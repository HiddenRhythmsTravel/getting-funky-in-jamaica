Getting Funky in Jamaica

January 14 – 18, 2027

A Bold New Groove: From Havana to Kingston

We have been working tirelessly behind the scenes to curate something truly extraordinary, and we are incredibly excited to announce that for January 2027, Getting Funky is heading to the absolute heart and soul of Caribbean rhythm: Kingston, Jamaica.

True to the Getting Funky tradition, this will be a deeply music-forward immersion. Together, we will dive into the roots of reggae, feel the baseline of the island, and connect our spirits to the vibrant energy of Jamaica.

Music Diplomacy in Action

A core heartbeat of this journey is our commitment to music diplomacy. This isn't just an exclusive getaway for our VIP travelers—it is a mission to serve and uplift the next generation of musical pioneers. On this trip, we are bridging cultures by bringing together youth from Cuba, students from the Trombone Shorty Foundation, and local young artists in Jamaica through the Marley Foundation.

Expect a phenomenal, soul-stirring experience packed with world-class music, continuous sun, infectious vibes, and unforgettable camaraderie.

We are hitting the ground running with our featured traveling families: Trombone Shorty, Cimafunk, and Primera Linea anchoring the mission. This is just the beginning—we will be announcing additional heavy-hitting artists as we get closer to the island.

Because we maintain an intimate, deep-dive atmosphere, this is a strictly limited-capacity engagement. Spots will fill up immediately.
